Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our video content tool ("we", "our", "the App") handles information when publishing translated video content to social media platforms.

1. Information We Collect

The App accesses your social media account only to publish video content that you have reviewed and approved. We access:

  • Basic account information (username, account ID) needed to publish content.
  • Authorization tokens provided by the platform to upload videos on your behalf.

We do not collect personal data from viewers or third parties.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to upload and publish video content that you approve. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Video files and translation data are stored locally on the user's own computer. Authorization tokens are stored securely and used only for publishing.

4. Third-Party Platforms

When content is published, it is subject to the privacy policies and terms of the respective platform (such as TikTok). We recommend reviewing those policies.

5. Data Retention

You may revoke the App's access at any time through your platform account settings. Upon revocation, the App can no longer access your account.
